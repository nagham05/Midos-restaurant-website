The Story

Welcome to mido’s, your go-to spot for delicious, fresh and satisfying meals since 2006! Founded by our passionate owner, we began our journey in the vibrant city of Saida, where we quickly became a favorite for our mouthwatering burgers, flavorful sandwiches, crisp salads and wholesome meal options.

With a focus on quality, taste and customer satisfaction, we’ve grown from a single location to opening our second branch in Antelias. At mido’s, we take pride in serving food that is prepared with love, using only the freshest ingredients and recipes that bring a unique twist to your favorite fast-food classics.

Whether you’re craving a juicy burger, a fresh salad or a hearty meal, we’ve got something for everyone. Join us and experience fast food done right, where every bite tells a story of passion and dedication.

We look forward to welcoming you to our Saida & Antelias branches and becoming your favorite spot for a quick, satisfying meal!
